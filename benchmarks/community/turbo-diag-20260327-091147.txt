  HOW TO READ THESE RESULTS
============================================

1. MODEL: Section 3 confirms which model and quantization you're running.

   across all depths. If it degrades = context scaling regression.

   Healthy (M5):  0.92x (short) -> 0.72x (48K) — gradual degradation
   Problem (M1):  0.90x (short) -> 0.09x (42K) — constant cache thrashing

   where turbo3 decode starts falling off a cliff.

6. THERMAL: Compare Sections 2 and 12. If CPU_Speed_Limit dropped
   during the test, results may be artificially low.

7. MEMORY: Section 11 shows KV cache sizing. If the system is near
   its recommendedMaxWorkingSetSize, swap pressure kills decode.
